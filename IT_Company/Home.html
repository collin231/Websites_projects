HOW TO USE THE WEBSITE

Troubleshooting Procedures

Firstly for the service help to be able to identify the problem, a few qustions need to be asked by the service desk. These questions can be located by clicking on Trouble Shoot tab on the navigation bar which will take you the page with the trouble shooting questions and once you fill in all the information click submit it will be sent to the service desk and a agent will get back to you as soon as possible, usally within 24 hours

Forms

This form is for the Tech Support to use to log support requests. This will help us get more information about the incident. This form can be located by clicking on Forms tab on the navigation bar which will take you the page that contains the form . Once the incident has been logged in just click Submit The response time may dependent on the Urgency of the incident.

Asset register

The asset register tab contains a document that will help us detemine which computers needs to be replaced and when. You can view this by by clicking on Assest Register tab on the navigation bar which will take you the page with all the information

Escalation

When dealing with an incident it a certain deparment fails to deal with the incident it is passed on to the next deparment. you can view our escalation process by clicking on Escalation tab on the navigation bar which will take you the page with all the information
