Our History And Where Are Going

Our NGO was founded in 1992 by Mr Peter Smith and his wife Mary smith. Their main goal was to help out chidlrens from non-color races during the time of apartheid hence our motto "we are all eqaul". That little NGO is now a wide world known and has even appeared on newspaper and on TV

Currently we own one building which can accomadate about 400 children but sadly the number of kids keep on increasing each year. This year we currently have 387 children with an increase of 89 from last year. Our goal by next year is able to get a second building so we can accomadate more and more children. Our organization is only currently oparting in south africa and we want to extend into other african countries so we can help as much as we can

Our Mission

our mission is to be an equal opportunity provider for the upliftment of the underprivileged children by providing them timely education, shelter, food and care.To be known as an organisation, with an innovative and robust approach, towards the upliftment of the needy and neglected children.To be abreast with rhyme and time in order to empower the underprivileged children and assist them to lead a dignified life. To inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children, and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.

Our Vision

Our Vision to reach the maximum needy,to be in reach of all the needy,be the most sought for,recognized and admired organisation,supporting the overall development of underprivileged children,To enable people to take responsibility for the situation of the deprived Indian child and so motivate them to seek resolution through individual and collective action thereby enabling children to realise their full potential, Build a world in which every child attains the right to survival, protection, development and participation.
